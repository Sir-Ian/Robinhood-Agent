## Questions

- Did high-confidence candidates outperform medium- and low-confidence candidates?
- Did candidates scoring 75+ outperform watchlist or rejected candidates?
- Were risk scores predictive of drawdown or invalidation?
- Were liquidity scores predictive of spread/slippage cost?
- Did portfolio-fit scores predict benchmark-relative improvement?
- Should the scoring policy become stricter, looser, or unchanged?

Agents may propose scoring policy changes, but may not autonomously loosen thresholds.
